What is GDP?

GDP (Gross Domestic Product) represents the total value of goods and services produced within a country. It is one of the most important indicators of economic strength and overall development.

Tracking GDP over time helps identify growth patterns, economic crises, and recovery periods. Sudden drops often reflect major disruptions, while steady increases indicate long-term stability and expansion.
